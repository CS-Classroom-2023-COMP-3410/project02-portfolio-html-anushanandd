About Me

I am a junior at the University of Denver pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and minors in Mathematics and Psychology.

I currently work as a research assistant for the Vision, Perception, and Emotional Cognition Lab on campus at the University of Denver. I design vision experiments utilizing eye-tracking and neural activity and analyze the data to investigate the responses and perception to different visual stimuli.

During the Fall of my Junior year, I studied abroad at Queen Mary University of London where I was able to immerse myself in a diverse environment and travel across Europe. I was also able to watch my favorite soccer team, Chelsea, play a few times.
